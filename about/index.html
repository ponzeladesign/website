Workflow

01

Research

Collect the personality and values of the brand. Contribute ideas for materials and style.

02

Ideation

Sketch the main ideas, outline how the product works and assess its advantages and disadvantages.

03

Rendering

Explain in more detail its method of use and its meaning. Show a first rendering of the product.

04

Prototyping

Evaluation of the prototypes, testing, error correction, and presentation of the final product.
